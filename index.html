Privacy Policy for Attendance Pro

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Attendance Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our attendance management application.

By using Attendance Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We collect the following personal information:

  • Name - To identify employees in the system
  • Email Address - For account authentication and communication
  • Employee ID - For internal identification
  • Phone Number - For contact purposes
  • Department - For organizational structure
  • Role - To determine access permissions (Admin, Manager, Employee)

2. Location Data

We collect precise location data when you mark attendance:

  • GPS Coordinates (Latitude and Longitude) - To verify you are at the correct work location
  • Address - Human-readable location information
Purpose: Location data is used for:
  • Geofencing validation (ensuring attendance is marked from authorized locations)
  • Attendance verification
  • Preventing fraudulent attendance marking

Location Collection:

  • Location is only collected when you actively mark attendance (check-in/check-out)
  • Location is NOT collected in the background
  • Location is NOT tracked continuously
  • You can deny location permission, but attendance marking will not work

3. Attendance Records

We collect and store:

  • Check-in Time - When you arrive at work
  • Check-out Time - When you leave work
  • Date - The date of attendance
  • Status - Present, Late, Absent
  • Comments - Optional notes you add when marking attendance
  • Attendance Corrections - Requests to fix attendance errors

4. Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device Model - For compatibility and troubleshooting
  • Operating System Version - For app optimization
  • App Version - For support purposes

5. Photos/Camera (Optional)

If face recognition is enabled by your organization:

  • Face Photos - Captured when marking attendance for identity verification
  • Purpose: To prevent buddy punching and ensure the correct person is marking attendance
  • Storage: Face data is processed and stored securely on your organization's server
  • Control: Your organization controls whether face recognition is required

How We Use Your Information

1. Attendance Management

  • Record and track employee attendance
  • Calculate working hours
  • Generate attendance reports
  • Monitor punctuality and presence

2. Location Verification

  • Verify employees are at authorized work locations
  • Enforce geofencing rules set by your organization
  • Prevent fraudulent attendance marking from unauthorized locations

3. Identity Verification

  • Authenticate users during login
  • Verify identity when marking attendance (if face recognition is enabled)
  • Prevent unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

1. Where Data is Stored

  • Your Organization's Server: All attendance data is stored on your organization's private server
  • Not Stored by Us: We do not store your data on our servers
  • Local Cache: Some data may be temporarily cached on your device for offline functionality

2. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication: Secure token-based authentication
  • Access Control: Role-based access (Admin, Manager, Employee)
  • Session Management: Automatic logout after inactivity
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive data is stored securely on your device

Data Sharing and Disclosure

1. Within Your Organization

Your data is shared with:

  • Administrators - Full access to manage the system
  • Managers - Access to their team's attendance data
  • HR Department - Access for payroll and HR purposes

2. Third Parties

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data may be shared only in these circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request
  • Organization's Service Providers: If your organization uses third-party services (e.g., cloud hosting)
  • With Your Consent: If you explicitly agree to share data

3. No Advertising

We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

1. Access Your Data

You have the right to:

  • View your attendance records
  • Access your profile information
  • Review your correction requests

2. Correct Your Data

You can:

  • Update your profile information
  • Request attendance corrections through the app
  • Contact your admin for data updates

3. Location Permissions

You can:

  • Grant or deny location permissions in device settings
  • Note: Denying location will prevent attendance marking

4. Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Contact your organization's administrator
  • Your organization controls data deletion policies
  • Some data may be retained for legal/compliance reasons

Children's Privacy

Attendance Pro is NOT intended for children under 13 years of age.
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • If you believe a child has provided information, contact your organization's admin
  • The app is designed for workplace attendance management only

Permissions Required

Android Permissions:

  1. Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)
    • Purpose: Mark attendance with location verification
    • When: Only when marking attendance
    • Required: Yes (for attendance marking)
  2. Camera (CAMERA)
    • Purpose: Capture face photo for identity verification
    • When: Only when marking attendance (if face recognition enabled)
    • Required: Optional (depends on organization settings)
  3. Internet (INTERNET)
    • Purpose: Communicate with organization's server
    • When: Always (for app functionality)
    • Required: Yes

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted in the app
  • Effective immediately upon posting
  • Notified to users if significant changes occur
